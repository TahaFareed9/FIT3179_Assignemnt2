Blood Donations in Malaysia

Blood donations are a critical lifeline for healthcare systems, ensuring the availability of a constant supply of essential blood components across Malaysia. Patients of numerous kinds of medical conidtions are in need of blood transfusions every day, and blood donations is one way they can receive the help the need.

Blood Donations by State (2023)

There exists some large regional disparites in blood donation levels, which may suggest the need for targeted efforts to raise awareness about the importance of donating blood in under-represented states such as Kelantan. This would increase the blood donaton levels in these states, and therefore creating a more stable supply of blood components.

Blood Donations in Selected States (2018-2023)

Blood donation trends overall remained stable over the last six years, with only a slight drop during the start of the COVID-19 pandemic. There is only one expeption to this trend, that being the state Kedah, which saw more fluctuation in blood donation levels, which could indicate the existence of an underlying issue that is worth investigating into.

Blood Donation Distribution by Blood Type in Selected States (2023)

The blood types that have the highest demand are O- and O+ due to O- blood type being a universal donor, and most blood types being able to receive blood transfusions of O+ type. Meanwhile, the blood types with the least demand are AB+ as it is a universal recipient, and AB- as it is able to receive many types of blood. This is clearly reflected in charts' blood donations type distribution, and it shows that most states are able to meet this demand.
